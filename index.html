 Adjust the Odometers

You can adjust the Odometer at any time after yo started your trail.

Double tap on the distance you want to change to enter the new distance or use the +/- buttons next to the odometers.

Reverse

Missed a turn?

Tap the Reverse Button (we call it Rewind!) and continue to drive until you are back on track! 

Night View

Driving in the right? The app has a special mode for it!

Enable “Night View” in settings and the app will change its color to be more readable in low light situation (dark background).
